# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

## Overview

Namibia Phrasebook is a guest-first mobile phrasebook for practical travel communication.
The current launch build is designed to let people browse bundled phrase content without
creating an account.

## What This Version Does

- Lets users browse phrase content in Afrikaans, Oshiwambo, and Nama-Damara
- Uses workbook-backed phrase content bundled into the app
- Does not require sign in for the current v1 experience

## Information We Collect In The Current Guest-Only Build

For the current launch build, the app does not require account registration for core phrase
browsing and is not intended to collect personal account data in order to use the main
phrasebook experience.

This v1 build does not currently rely on:

- user account creation
- payment collection
- advertising
- advertising identifiers
- third-party ad tracking
- analytics SDKs

## Information Stored On Your Device

To support the app experience, Namibia Phrasebook may store limited app data locally on
your device, such as:

- favorite phrases
- recent phrases you opened
- phrases you saved for a trip
- quick-practice progress
- phrase-report status inside the app

This information is stored locally on the device to support app features and is not used
to create a user account in the current launch build.

## Permissions

The current guest-only build does not require camera, microphone, contacts, photo library,
or location permissions for its main phrasebook experience.

## Offline Content

The phrasebook ships with bundled workbook content so the core phrase browsing experience
can work offline after installation.

## Support And Feedback

If you choose to contact support or use a phrase-reporting link, you may provide personal
information such as your email address and any details you include in your message. That
information is only used to review and respond to support requests or phrase feedback.

If you use the app through TestFlight or another Apple beta-testing service, Apple may also
collect and process crash logs, diagnostic information, and feedback according to Apple’s
own policies and services.

## Changes In Later Releases

If a later release adds accounts, analytics, purchases, or other data collection, this
policy should be updated before that release is submitted to the App Store or Play Store.

## Support

Support contact:

- `support@namibiaphrasebook.app`

If a different public support inbox is used for launch, this policy should be updated to
match the final contact address.
