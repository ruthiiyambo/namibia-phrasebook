# Reviewer Notes

## App Review Summary

Namibia Phrasebook is a guest-first travel phrasebook focused on practical communication in
Namibia. The launch build allows phrase browsing without creating an account.

## What Reviewers Should Know

- No sign in or sign up is required in this v1 build.
- Phrase content is bundled into the app from the workbook source and is available without
  account setup.
- The current release does not expose purchases, subscriptions, or locked-content flows.
- The current guest-only build is focused on browsing, category navigation, and search.

## Main User Flows

1. Open the app.
2. Pick one of the included languages.
3. Browse by category or use Quick Find search.
4. Open phrase cards for pronunciation and usage notes.
